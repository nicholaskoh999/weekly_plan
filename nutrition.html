Daily Meals

Breakfast / 早餐 Protein first: eggs, Greek yogurt, whey, tofu, or chicken.先吃蛋白質：雞蛋、希臘 yogurt、乳清、豆腐或雞肉。

Add one easy carb if work feels low energy: oats, bread, banana, or rice.如果上班容易沒力，加一份燕麥、麵包、香蕉或飯。

Protein Daily
Lunch / 午餐 Best plate: 1 palm protein, 1 fist carbs, 2 fists vegetables.最穩：一掌蛋白質、一拳碳水、兩拳菜。

Example: chicken rice with less oil, eggs + bread, chicken noodles, or tofu + rice.例子：少油雞飯、雞蛋配麵包、雞肉麵、豆腐配飯。

Protein Carbs
Dinner / 晚餐 Eat 60-90 min before training so your stomach is not too full.訓練前 60-90 分鐘吃，胃不要太撐。

Keep it simple: rice + chicken, noodles + eggs, tofu + rice, or oats + whey.簡單就好：飯配雞、麵配蛋、豆腐配飯、燕麥配乳清。

Energy Digest
Night / 晚上 After training, do not turn Netflix time into snack time.訓練後不要把 Netflix 變成零食時間。

If hungry: whey, yogurt, eggs, tofu, or fruit.如果真的餓：乳清、yogurt、雞蛋、豆腐或水果。

Fixed 100-Day Meal Base / 固定 100 天飲食模板 Repeat this weekly during the 100-day cut. Keep the meal base fixed, then rotate small Korean, Japanese, or Thai flavors when you want variety.100 天減脂期間每週重複這個模板；主食和蛋白質固定，想換口味時可以少量輪換韓式、日式或泰式味道。

Monday to Friday lunch follows your real routine: one pulled chicken can day, one tuna can day, two economy rice days, and Thursday family lunch outside.星期一到五午餐照你的真實習慣：1 天雞肉絲罐頭、1 天 tuna 罐頭、2 天經濟飯 / 雜菜飯、星期四跟家人外食。

Chilled + Grocery / 冷藏與乾貨 Look for tofu, plain Greek yogurt, low fat milk, unsweetened soy milk, whole wheat bread, rice, Ayam Brand Tuna in Water, and Ayam Brand Pulled Chicken.找豆腐、原味希臘酸奶、低脂牛奶、無糖豆奶、全麥麵包、米、Ayam Brand 水浸 tuna 和 Ayam Brand 雞肉絲罐頭。

Buy one pulled chicken can and one tuna can for Monday to Friday lunch. Choose plain yogurt, low sugar drinks, and basic rice.買 1 罐雞肉絲和 1 罐 tuna 給星期一到五午餐用；選原味 yogurt、低糖飲料和普通米。

Seasoning Limit / 調味限制 Use light soy, black pepper, garlic, and only a small amount of gochujang or sesame oil.用生抽、黑胡椒、蒜；韓式辣醬和麻油只放少量。

If a canned item is not available, replace it with cooked chicken breast from home prep.如果罐頭買不到，就用自己預先煮好的雞胸代替。

Economy Rice Cut Guide / 雜菜飯減脂拿法 Take half rice, one lean protein, and two vegetables. Avoid curry gravy, fried chicken skin, fried egg, creamy sauce, and oily vegetables.拿半飯、一份瘦蛋白、兩份菜；避開咖喱汁、炸雞皮、煎蛋、奶油醬和很油的菜。

Best choices: steamed chicken, braised tofu, egg tofu only sometimes, cabbage, cucumber, broccoli, mushrooms, or clear soup vegetables.好選擇：白切雞 / 蒸雞、滷豆腐、蛋豆腐偶爾、包菜、黃瓜、西蘭花、菇類或清湯菜。

Training Day

Before Training / 訓練前 Do not train empty if you feel weak. 會沒力就不要空腹練。

Small option: banana + whey, bread + eggs, or rice + lean meat. 小份選擇：香蕉加乳清、麵包加蛋、飯加瘦肉。

After Training / 訓練後 Hydrate first, then check total protein for the day. 先補水，再看今天蛋白質夠不夠。

You do not need a huge late meal. You need repeatable recovery. 不需要很大份宵夜，需要的是每天可重複的恢復。

Simple Target / 簡單目標 For 77 kg, aim roughly 120-150 g protein per day if digestion is ok. 77kg 可以先抓每天約 120-150g 蛋白質，腸胃可以接受就好。

When weight drops, keep protein steady and reduce snacks or oily food first. 體重下降時蛋白質保持，先減零食和油的份量。

Nutrition Rules

80% Repeatable / 80% 可重複 Do not chase perfect dieting. Build meals you can repeat on work days. 不用追求完美飲食，先建立上班日能重複的吃法。
Drink Calories Down / 少喝熱量 Reduce milk tea, soda, sweet coffee, and alcohol first. 先減奶茶、汽水、甜咖啡和酒。
Adjust Slowly / 慢慢調整 If weight does not move for 2 weeks, reduce one small carb or snack portion. 兩星期沒變化，就少一小份碳水或零食。
