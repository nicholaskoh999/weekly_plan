Daily Meals

4 Meals
🍳 Breakfast / 早餐
Protein Daily
Protein first: eggs, Greek yogurt, whey, tofu, or chicken. 先吃蛋白質：雞蛋、希臘 yogurt、乳清、豆腐或雞肉。 Add one easy carb if work energy feels low: oats, bread, banana, or rice. 如果上班容易沒力，加一份燕麥、麵包、香蕉或飯。
🥗 Lunch / 午餐
Protein Carbs
Best plate: 1 palm of protein, 1 fist of carbs, 2 fists of vegetables. 最穩：一掌蛋白質、一拳碳水、兩拳菜。 Example: chicken rice with less oil, eggs + bread, chicken noodles, or tofu + rice. 例子：少油雞飯、雞蛋配麵包、雞肉麵、豆腐配飯。
🍚 Dinner / 晚餐
Energy Digest
Eat 60–90 minutes before training so your stomach is not too full. 訓練前 60-90 分鐘吃，胃不要太撐。 Keep it simple: rice + chicken, noodles + eggs, tofu + rice, or oats + whey. 簡單就好：飯配雞、麵配蛋、豆腐配飯、燕麥配乳清。
🌙 Night / 晚上
Control Recovery
After training, do not turn Netflix time into snack time. 訓練後不要把 Netflix 變成零食時間。 If hungry: whey, Greek yogurt, eggs, tofu, or fruit. 如果真的餓：乳清、yogurt、雞蛋、豆腐或水果。

🛒 AEON Grocery Picks Fresh Foods · Chilled · Dry Goods ⌄
Fresh Foods / 生鮮食品 Look in Fresh Foods for chicken breast, eggs, vegetables, fruits, and mushrooms.在 Fresh Foods 找雞胸、雞蛋、蔬菜、水果和菇類。

Buy chicken breast or chicken fillet, eggs, broccoli, cabbage, cucumber, lettuce or romaine, mushrooms, apples, and bananas.買雞胸或雞柳、雞蛋、西蘭花、包菜、黃瓜、生菜或羅馬生菜、菇、蘋果和香蕉。

Chilled + Grocery / 冷藏與乾貨 Look for tofu, plain Greek yogurt, low-fat milk, unsweetened soy milk, whole wheat bread, rice, Ayam Brand Tuna in Water, and Ayam Brand Pulled Chicken.找豆腐、原味希臘酸奶、低脂牛奶、無糖豆奶、全麥麵包、米、Ayam Brand 水浸 tuna 和 Ayam Brand 雞肉絲罐頭。

Buy one pulled chicken can and one tuna can for Monday to Friday lunch. Choose plain Greek yogurt, low-sugar drinks, and basic rice.買 1 罐雞肉絲和 1 罐 tuna 給星期一到五午餐用；選原味 yogurt、低糖飲料和普通米。

🍚 Economy Rice Cut Guide 雜菜飯減脂拿法 ⌄
Economy Rice Cut Guide / 雜菜飯減脂拿法 Take half rice, one lean protein, and two vegetables. Avoid curry gravy, fried chicken skin, fried egg, creamy sauce, and oily vegetables.拿半飯、一份瘦蛋白、兩份菜；避開咖喱汁、炸雞皮、煎蛋、奶油醬和很油的菜。

Best choices: steamed chicken, braised tofu, egg tofu only sometimes, cabbage, cucumber, broccoli, mushrooms, or clear soup vegetables.好選擇：白切雞 / 蒸雞、滷豆腐、蛋豆腐偶爾、包菜、黃瓜、西蘭花、菇類或清湯菜。

🧂 Seasoning Limit 調味限制 / Fallback Guide ⌄
Seasoning Limit / 調味限制 Use light soy, black pepper, garlic, and only a small amount of gochujang or sesame oil.用生抽、黑胡椒、蒜；韓式辣醬和麻油只放少量。

If a canned item is not available, replace it with cooked chicken breast from home prep.如果罐頭買不到，就用自己預先煮好的雞胸代替。

💪 Training Day Nutrition Before · After · Protein Target ⌄
Before Training / 訓練前 Do not train empty if you feel weak. 會沒力就不要空腹練。

Small option: banana + whey, bread + eggs, or rice + lean meat. 小份選擇：香蕉加乳清、麵包加蛋、飯加瘦肉。

After Training / 訓練後 Hydrate first, then check total protein for the day. 先補水，再看今天蛋白質夠不夠。

You do not need a huge late meal. You need repeatable recovery. 不需要很大份宵夜，需要的是每天可重複的恢復。

Simple Target / 簡單目標 For 77 kg, aim roughly 120–150 g protein per day if digestion feels okay. 77kg 可以先抓每天約 120–150 g 蛋白質，腸胃可以接受就好。

When weight drops, keep protein steady and reduce snacks or oily food first. 體重下降時蛋白質保持，先減零食和油的份量。

📋 Fuel Rules 80% Repeatable · Drink Less · Adjust Slowly ⌄
80% Repeatable / 80% 可重複 Do not chase perfect dieting. Build meals you can repeat on workdays. 不用追求完美飲食，先建立上班日能重複的吃法。
Drink Fewer Calories / 少喝熱量 Reduce milk tea, soda, sweet coffee, and alcohol first. 先減奶茶、汽水、甜咖啡和酒。
Adjust Slowly / 慢慢調整 If weight does not move for 2 weeks, reduce one small carb or snack portion. 兩星期沒變化，就少一小份碳水或零食。
