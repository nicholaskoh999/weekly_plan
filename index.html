Sunday Stretching Guide / 星期日拉伸 Guide
20–30 min recovery / 恢复拉伸
›
Hamstring Stretch / 后腿拉伸
2 × 30 sec each side
腿伸直，身体慢慢往前，不要弹动。
Keep leg straight and lean forward slowly.
Kneeling Hip Flexor / 髋屈肌拉伸
2 × 30 sec each side
一脚前一脚后，骨盆轻轻往前推。
Lunge position, push hips forward gently.
Chest + Front Shoulder Stretch / 胸 + 前肩拉伸
2 × 30 sec
胸口打开，改善圆肩和久坐紧绷。
Open chest, useful for rounded shoulders.
Shoulder Stretch / 肩膀拉伸
2 × 30 sec each side
手臂横过胸前，另一只手轻轻拉住。
Pull arm across chest gently.
Cat Stretch / 猫式下背拉伸
2 × 30 sec
慢慢拱背和放松，不要硬压腰。
Round and relax your back slowly.
