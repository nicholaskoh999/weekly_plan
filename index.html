Gym Plan

Run this as a 100-day shape plan from 22 June 2026. The goal is not random hard training; it is repeated weekly structure that makes your shoulders wider, back cleaner, chest fuller, waist tighter, and clothes fit better.

Weekly FocusMon chest + shoulder, Tue back + biceps, Wed swimming, Thu shoulder + abs, Fri upper-body V-shape.

Equipment StrategyUse dumbbells for stable strength and shape work. Use ES3000 for smooth cable tension on lats, rear delts, side delts, curls, and triceps.

Progress TargetKeep form clean first. When the top rep range feels controlled, add a little weight or one extra rep next time.

DB ES3000 Core Swim Warm

Rules

Clean Reps Only

No swinging, no rushing, no ego weight. If form breaks, the set is done.

Control The Negative

Lower the weight slowly for 2-3 seconds. That is where shape and muscle control are built.

Rest With Purpose

Rest 60-90 sec for raises, curls, abs, and pushdowns. Rest 90-150 sec for presses, rows, and pulldowns.

Recover To Grow

Sleep, protein, walking, and swimming matter. The body changes between workouts, not only during workouts.
